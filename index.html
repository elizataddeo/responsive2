Meet the Designer

Hi, My name is Elizabeth Taddeo. I am a senior Media Arts and Design major at James Madison University.

As a passionate media designer, I specialize in crafting visually captivating and user-centric experiences across diverse platforms. My expertise encompasses a wide range of media design disciplines, including graphic design, web design, and photography.

I am adept at utilizing industry-standard tools and software to bring creative visions to life, such as Adobe Creative Suite, Figma, and Final Cut Pro. My design philosophy centers around clarity, consistency, and user experience, ensuring that every project I undertake is both visually stunning and highly effective.

Graphic Design

My journey into the world of design began with a fascination for the power of imagery and typography. Through rigorous training and hands-on experience, I have honed my skills in Adobe Creative Suite, mastering tools like Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign.

Photography

I am dedicated to capturing the unique beauty in every subject, whether it's a bustling city street, a serene natural landscape, or a captivating human portrait. Through my lens, I aim to inspire and connect with others through the power of visual storytelling.
